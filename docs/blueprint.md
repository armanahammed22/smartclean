# **App Name**: QuickOrder

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog Display: Browse products in a modern grid layout, showing product image, name, price, short description, and an 'Add to Cart' button.
- Detailed Product Pages: View comprehensive product details, full description, price, quantity selector, and 'Add to Cart' functionality on individual product pages.
- Shopping Cart Management: Add, remove, and update product quantities in the cart, with real-time subtotal and total price calculation, accessible via a top navigation icon.
- Customer Checkout Form: Collect essential customer details like name, phone number, email (optional), delivery address, and order notes during checkout.
- Order Submission & Confirmation: Submit finalized order details to the ERP backend API and display an instant order success message with a unique Order ID to the customer.
- AI-Powered Product Copy Enhancer Tool: A tool to generate marketing copy or rephrase descriptions for products, aiming for clarity and SEO optimization on the public-facing ordering page.

## Style Guidelines:

- Light color scheme to convey a clean, professional, and accessible feel.
- Primary accent color: Professional Blue (#2263C0), chosen for its association with trustworthiness and efficiency in a clean interface.
- Background color: Desaturated light blue (#EDF3F8), providing a subtle yet cohesive backdrop to the primary color.
- Accent color: Fresh Cyan (#000080), an analogous hue providing a crisp highlight for interactive elements and calls to action, maintaining the overall clean aesthetic.
- Primary and body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), selected for its modern, highly legible, and objective aesthetic, ensuring readability across all devices.
- Use clean, minimalistic line-based icons for navigation elements and product actions, such as a shopping cart icon in the top navigation.
- Products displayed in a responsive, modern grid layout, adapting seamlessly for both mobile and desktop screens.
- Implement subtle, quick animations for adding items to the cart, opening product detail modals, and form submission to enhance user experience without distraction.
